Follow this simple guide to set up Call Track for your business. No technical expertise required.

1

Create Your Account

Visit our website and sign up with your business email.

  1. Go to miniclickcrm.com/signup
  2. Enter your business name and email
  3. Create a strong password
  4. Verify your email address
Create Account
2

Configure Your Organization

Set up your business profile in the dashboard.

  1. Log in to your dashboard
  2. Go to Settings → Organization
  3. Upload your company logo (optional)
  4. Set your timezone for accurate reporting
3

Add Your Employees

Invite team members to start tracking their calls.

Generate a 6-digit code and share it with the employee verbally.

For Employees

1

Download the App

Get "Call Track & Manage by MiniClick" from the Play Store.

Download for Android
2

Enter Your Login Code

Use the code or credentials provided by your manager.

Example Code Format:

XC-9921

3

Grant Permissions

Allow the app to access necessary features. This is required for call tracking.

Phone Calls
Call Logs
Microphone
Contacts
4

Disable Battery Optimization

This is critical to ensure the app runs in the background.

Important: Go to Settings → Apps → Call Manage → Battery → Set to "Unrestricted". This prevents Android from killing the app to save battery.

5

You're All Set!

The app will now automatically track your calls. Look for the "MiniClick is active" notification.

Tracking is now active

Common Issues

App is not recording calls

Make sure Microphone permission is granted and battery optimization is disabled.

Calls are not syncing to cloud

Check your internet connection. If "Wi-Fi Only" mode is ON, connect to Wi-Fi to sync.

App keeps stopping

Xiaomi, Samsung, and Oppo phones have aggressive battery savers. Enable "Autostart" in phone settings.
