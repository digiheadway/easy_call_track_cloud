Core Capabilities

Total visibility into
your phone communications

SIM-Based Auto Tracking

No VOIP required. MiniClick tracks native calls directly from your Android phone's SIM card. Works perfectly with Dual-SIM devices.

Automatic Recording

Every conversation is recorded and automatically attached to the call log. Recordings are compressed for efficient cloud storage.

Instant Cloud Sync

Logs and recordings are synced to your manager dashboard the moment a call hangs up. Full transparency for field teams.

Powerful Search & Filters

Find any call in seconds. Filter by contact name, number, employee, date, duration, or even call type (Incoming/Outgoing/Missed).

Queue Management

No internet? No problem. MiniClick queues all data locally and syncs it automatically once your connection is back.

Advanced Privacy

You control what gets synced. Exclude personal contacts, family numbers, or specific SIM slots from tracking easily.
