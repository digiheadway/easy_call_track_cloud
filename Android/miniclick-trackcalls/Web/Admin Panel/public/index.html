Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about MiniClick Calls.

MiniClick Calls works on all Android devices running Android 8.0 (Oreo) and above. We recommend disabling battery optimization for the best experience. iOS is currently not supported due to Apple's system restrictions on call recording.
Call recording laws vary by country and state. Generally, you should inform the other party that the call is being recorded. Some jurisdictions require "one-party consent" while others require "all-party consent." Always consult your local laws to ensure compliance.
Yes! MiniClick Calls fully supports Dual-SIM devices. You can choose to track calls from both SIMs or exclude specific numbers and SIM slots in the app settings.
Yes. Calls and recordings are queued locally on the device and will automatically sync to the cloud as soon as an internet connection (Wi-Fi or Mobile Data) is restored.
Currently, MiniClick Calls specializes in standard cellular calls. Recording for VoIP apps like WhatsApp, Telegram, or Zoom is a feature on our roadmap but is not supported in the current version.
Your privacy is our priority. All call metadata and recordings are encrypted during transit and at rest in our secure cloud storage. Only authorized users within your organization can access the recordings through the Admin Panel.
