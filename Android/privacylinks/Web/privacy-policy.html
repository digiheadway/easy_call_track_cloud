Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 05, 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to Private Files Access & Share, developed by DigiHeadway. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application and related services.

2. Data We Collect

We collect information to provide better services to all our users. This includes:

  • Personal Information: When you sign in (e.g., via Google Sign-In), we collect your name, email address, and profile picture to create your account.
  • User Content: Files, folders, and links you choose to upload, store, or share through our service.
  • Usage Data: Information about how you use the app, such as content interactions, points earned/spent, and feature usage.
  • Device & Technical Data: IP address, device model, operating system version, and Advertising IDs (e.g., Google AAID).

3. Third-Party Services & Ads

We use trusted third-party services to support our application, which may collect and process data according to their own privacy policies:

  • Google AdMob & Facebook Audience Network: We display advertisements to support the free version of our app. These services may use your Advertising ID and other device data to show personalized ads.
  • Google Play Billing: We use Google Play's billing system for in-app purchases and subscriptions. We do not store your financial or credit card information on our servers.
  • Firebase: Used for secure authentication, database storage, and analytics.

4. App Permissions & Security Features

Our app requires certain permissions and implements specific security measures to function:

  • Internet Access: Required to upload/download content and verify ad/subscription status.
  • Screenshot Protection: To protect the privacy of shared content, our app actively prevents screen capture (screenshots/screen recording) on sensitive screens.
  • Advertising ID Permission: Requested to provide relevant advertising experiences.

5. How We Use Your Data

We use your data strictly to:

  • Facilitate secure file and link sharing.
  • Manage your "Points" balance and Premium status.
  • Prevent fraud and enforce our Terms of usage.
  • Improve app performance and user experience.

6. Data Retention & Deletion

We retain your personal data only as long as necessary to provide our services. You can request the deletion of your account and associated data at any time via the "Delete Data" section in our app/website or by contacting us.

7. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, please contact us at support@pokipro.com.
