How to save money when shifting

This section will discuss how to save money on moving costs and how you can still hire professional movers.


Moving during the Off-Season

Moving companies usually raise their rates between May and August. This is when moving companies are most busy. Moving outside these dates can help you save money. Movers tend to be less busy, and their rates will drop.

Move during the Week

People needing moving services the most are busier on weekends, particularly towards the end. These peak times are when many movers charge higher, so moving during the week is worth considering. Or ask your moving company whether they will discount your rate if your move takes place during the week.

Review the Right Moving Company & Get Hired

Local movers usually charge per hour. If you look at reviews, you can gauge the speed of a moving company. You should be aware that slow-moving companies can take longer to complete your move and may cost you more. Look for movers who have positive reviews and move items efficiently and quickly.

Ask your moving company for a fixed rate

Ask your local moving company for a fixed price if you are moving locally.
